# Attribution — vendored design-language exemplars

The worked exemplar(s) in this folder are **seeded from** the
[`awesome-design-md`](https://github.com/voltagent/awesome-design-md) collection
(VoltAgent), licensed **MIT**. That project extracts publicly visible CSS/design
values from real websites into Google Stitch `DESIGN.md` files; it claims no ownership
of any brand's visual identity, and neither do we.

| Exemplar | Seeded from | Upstream | License |
|---|---|---|---|
| `linear.design.md` | Linear's public marketing surface | `voltagent/awesome-design-md` → `design-md/linear.app/DESIGN.md` | MIT |

**What we changed.** Each exemplar here is **condensed** (a representative token subset,
not a verbatim copy) and **extended with the AI-Forward floors** that the raw Stitch
format omits — a WCAG contrast audit at the token layer (U16), the complete component
state set incl. empty/loading/error (U9), theme/density modes (U4), the paired UI
Archetype Signature (G11), motion + reduced-motion (U10), and a performance budget
(U17). These additions are the pack's; the seed vocabulary is upstream's.

**Adapt, don't clone (U12).** These exist to demonstrate the pack's design-language
artifact and as *starting points to specialize* — never to ship a pixel clone of
another company's product. Honor the familiar pattern, then make it yours.

Brand names and visual identities are the property of their respective owners.
